Our Services

At OG Movers, we offer a comprehensive range of moving and packing services designed to make your transition as smooth and stress-free as possible. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to handling your belongings with the utmost care and efficiency. Below are the services we provide:

1. Residential Moving

Moving to a new home? Our residential moving services are tailored to meet your specific needs, whether you're relocating across town or across the country. We ensure that your possessions are safely and securely transported to your new home.

2. Commercial Moving

Relocating your business? Our commercial moving services minimize downtime and ensure a seamless transition. We handle everything from office furniture to sensitive equipment, allowing you to focus on your business.

3. Packing Services

Need help with packing? Our packing services include providing high-quality packing materials and expert packing techniques to protect your belongings during transit. We handle everything from delicate items to large furniture.

4. Loading and Unloading

Short on manpower? Our team can assist with the heavy lifting. We provide professional loading and unloading services to ensure your items are properly secured and safely transferred to and from the moving vehicle.

5. Long-Distance Moving

Moving far away? Our long-distance moving services are designed to handle the complexities of interstate or cross-country relocations. We provide reliable and efficient transportation to ensure your items arrive on time and in perfect condition.

6. Specialty Item Moving

Have valuable or fragile items? We specialize in moving delicate and high-value items such as pianos, antiques, and artwork. Our team uses specialized equipment and techniques to ensure the safe transport of your precious belongings.

FAQs

1. What services does your moving company offer?

We offer a range of services including local and long-distance moving, packing and unpacking, furniture disassembly and reassembly, storage solutions, and more.

2. How do I get a quote for my move?

You can get a free quote by filling out our online form, calling our customer service team, or scheduling an in-home estimate with one of our representatives.

3. Are your movers trained and insured?

Yes, all our movers are trained professionals who are fully licensed and insured to ensure the safety of your belongings during the move.

4. Do you provide packing materials, and can I pack my own items?

We offer high-quality packing materials for purchase. You can choose to pack your own items or opt for our professional packing services for added convenience.

5. What should I do to prepare for moving day?

We recommend decluttering and organizing your belongings beforehand. Ensure all boxes are labeled clearly, and provide any special instructions to our team for fragile or valuable items.

6. Do you offer storage options if my new home isn’t ready yet?

Yes, we provide short-term and long-term storage solutions in our secure facilities to accommodate your needs until your new home is ready for move-in.

7. Can you handle specialty items such as pianos, antiques, or large appliances?

Absolutely, our movers are trained to handle specialty items with care. Please inform us in advance so we can make appropriate arrangements.

8. What happens if my belongings are damaged during the move?

While we take every precaution to ensure a safe move, in the rare event of damage, we have a claims process in place. Our customer service team will assist you through the process.

9. How far in advance should I schedule my move?

It’s best to schedule your move as soon as you have a confirmed moving date, especially during peak seasons like summer. This helps ensure availability and allows time for proper planning.

10. Do you offer moving services on weekends and holidays?

Yes, we offer moving services seven days a week, including weekends and holidays, to accommodate your schedule and make your move as convenient as possible.
